What is Coffee?

Coffee, a staple beverage among college students around the earth, finds its roots in the coffee bean. This caffeine-infused drink assumes the role of a reliable companion during late-night study sessions and serves as an aid in maintaining alertness during early morning classes the next day. However, it is crucial to acknowledge that, for some students, the allure of its stimulating properties can lead to a cycle of dependence, often concealed beneath the inviting ambiance of coffee shops, the camaraderie of social gatherings, and the pursuit of picturesque moments.
